Accessibility

We are committed to ensuring our experiences and digital presence are accessible to everyone.

Our Commitment

LK Management is dedicated to providing inclusive meet & greet experiences for all fans. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to create unforgettable memories, regardless of physical ability, sensory needs, or other accessibility requirements.

Event Accessibility

We work closely with each venue to accommodate a wide range of needs, including but not limited to:

  • Mobility access — Wheelchair-accessible routes, reserved seating, and companion attendance where needed
  • Sensory accommodations — Quiet spaces, advance notice of loud music or lighting effects, and flexible scheduling where possible
  • Visual and hearing support — Assistance with orientation, clear signage, and communication support
  • Medical needs — Access to medical equipment, service animals, and on-site first aid

Please detail any accessibility needs in the "Special Requests" field when submitting your booking inquiry. Our team reviews every request individually and coordinates with venue staff to ensure appropriate arrangements are in place before your arrival.

Digital Accessibility

We strive to make this website accessible to all visitors. Our design choices include:

  • High-contrast color schemes for readability
  • Keyboard-navigable forms and links
  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Responsive design that adapts to various screen sizes and devices
  • Clear, legible typography with adequate spacing

If you encounter any difficulty using this website or need information in an alternative format, please contact us and we will assist you promptly.

Companion & Caregiver Policy

Guests who require a personal assistant, caregiver, or companion to attend the event may request a complimentary companion ticket. Please include this request in your booking form. Documentation is not required, but advance notice helps us coordinate with venue security and seating arrangements.

Accessible Communication

If you prefer to communicate via phone, video call, email, or written correspondence rather than through our online form, we are happy to accommodate. Contact us at lennykravitzmanagementteam07@gmail.com and let us know your preferred method of contact.

Feedback & Improvements

We continuously seek to improve our accessibility efforts. If you have suggestions, feedback, or concerns about accessibility at our events or on our website, please reach out. Your input helps us create better experiences for all fans.

Contact Our Accessibility Coordinator

For specific accessibility questions or to discuss your needs before booking, email us at lennykravitzmanagementteam07@gmail.com with "Accessibility" in the subject line. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
